本課程旨在探索生成式AI在教學中的多元應用，涵蓋文字、圖像、影音等領域，並結合設計思考與創意發想。課程分為理論介紹與實際演練，學生將學習如何運用生成式AI工具優化標題、撰寫新聞稿、進行圖像創作與影片製作等。透過案例分享與小組討論，學生不僅能夠理解AI在不同領域的應用，也能在期末報告中提出AI應用於幸福校園的具體構想，深化生成式AI對教育創新的實踐。
This course aims to explore the diverse applications of generative AI in education, covering areas such as text, images, and multimedia, while integrating design thinking and creative brainstorming. The course includes both theoretical introduction and hands-on practice, where students will learn how to use generative AI tools to optimize titles, write press releases, create images, and produce videos. Through case studies and group discussions, students will not only understand AI applications in various fields but also present concrete ideas on applying AI to create a happier campus in their final projects, deepening the practical use of AI in educational innovation.
